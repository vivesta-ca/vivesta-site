PEFSAD (Aide domestique)

Financial assistance program in Quebec that helps reduce the cost of eligible domestic help, such as house cleaning and some daily living tasks for seniors.

Depending on your situation and income, the hourly rate for approved services may be reduced significantly compared to paying entirely out of pocket.

Official information: RAMQ – Aide domestique (PEFSAD)

Tax credit for home-support services for seniors

Quebec offers a refundable tax credit for certain home-support services for people aged 70 and over, which can apply to housekeeping, meal preparation, and similar services.

The amount depends on your age, family income, and eligible expenses. It is usually claimed when you file your provincial income tax return.

Official information: See Revenu Québec’s page on the tax credit for home-support services for seniors .
